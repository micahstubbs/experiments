Sold at auction 19 March 2016 $1,700,000

45 Alamein Avenue, Ashburton VIC 3147

3 Beds 1 Bath 2 Parking
  • Land area 820 sqm

Promise of a bright future

On a generous allotment of 820sqm approx., this original 1940s home with north facing rear is poised for its purchaser to create an inviting family home in a sought after location, with options for immediate enjoyment, contemporary updating, extension or redevelopment with views over the valley. STCA. Comfortable current day spaces include large living room, three spacious bedrooms, tiled bathroom, central kitchen with Blanco oven, meals area and rear family room with sliding doors to a wraparound deck and deep rear garden. Features air-conditioning, ducted heating, 2nd WC. With a position close to Warner Reserve, Chadstone SC, Holmesglen, Village shops, train stations, freeway access, cafes, schools, bike trails, Ashburton Pool and Recreation Centre, its future is bright.
